# Background Image Prompts for Timeline Drift

The following prompts can be used in Gemini, Midjourney, or DALL-E to generate the background assets for the game.

## Instructions
1.  Generate the images using the prompts below.
2.  Save them in a folder named `assets/img` in the project root.
3.  Ensure filenames match (`default.jpg`, `infra_01.jpg`, etc.).
4.  Recommended Aspect Ratio: 16:9.

---

### 1. Default / Start Screen
**Filename:** `default.jpg`
**Prompt:**
> Abstract dark timeline void, floating particles, neon cyan timeline flowing into distance, cinematic sci-fi background, depth of field, dark charcoal and black aesthetics, subtle glow.

### 2. Infrastructure Maintenance
**Filename:** `infra_01.jpg`
**Prompt:**
> Futuristic city suspension bridge at night requiring maintenance, cyberpunk aesthetic, dark atmosphere, massive scale, neon warning lights, rain texture, high contrast, cinematic lighting, dark blue and grey tones.

### 3. Algorithmic Governance
**Filename:** `ai_01.jpg`
**Prompt:**
> High tech surveillance control room view, holographic city map projection, artificial intelligence interface overlay, dark blue and purple tones, cinematic, clean glass textures, data streams.

### 4. Energy Crisis
**Filename:** `energy_01.jpg`
**Prompt:**
> Massive futuristic power plant energy core at night, glowing red and orange embers contrasting with dark industrial machinery, billowing smoke, dark sci-fi atmosphere, volumetric lighting.

### 5. Genetic Enhancement
**Filename:** `bio_01.jpg`
**Prompt:**
> Futuristic medical laboratory interior, DNA double helix hologram in center, gene editing interface, dark clean aesthetic, glowing neon blue and cyan lights, glass panels, cinematic depth.
